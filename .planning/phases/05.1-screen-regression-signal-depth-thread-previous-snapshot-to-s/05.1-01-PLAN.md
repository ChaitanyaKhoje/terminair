<objective>
Wire the previous-snapshot parameter through to all three RegressionAnalyzer call sites (TD-01) and fix the clock that never fires on first TUI launch (TD-02).

Purpose: Three of the six regression signal types (grain_added, grain_removed, upstream_schema_change) are permanently silent because no screen passes a `previous` argument to RegressionAnalyzer. The clock status bar on ModelListScreen shows a stale/empty time until the user navigates away and back. Both bugs are wiring gaps — the underlying logic already exists and is tested.

Output: Two new async methods (get_previous_models on StateAggregator and MockDataProvider), _load_models overrides in three screens that fetch and store self._previous_models, updated RegressionAnalyzer call sites that pass previous=self._previous_models, _update_header() called once in on_mount, and five new unit tests covering the new methods and the three previously-unreachable signal types via the mock provider.
</objective>
